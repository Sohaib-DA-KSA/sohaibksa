## Design & Technical Details

- **Color palette:** White background, dark text, blue/teal (#0EA5E9 / #14B8A6) accents
- **Font:** Inter (clean, modern)
- **Fully responsive** — desktop, tablet, mobile
- **Subtle animations:** hover effects on cards and buttons, smooth scroll
- **Profile photo** embedded using the uploaded image
- **Single-page layout** with smooth scrolling between sections
- No backend needed — fully static frontend
